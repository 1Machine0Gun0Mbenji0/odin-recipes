LASAGNA

DESCRIPTION

The dish is a beautifully plated, classic lasagna served on a white ceramic plate. The lasagna slice is tall and neatly layered, showcasing multiple sheets of pasta alternating with rich, meaty tomato sauce and melted cheese. The top is golden-brown and slightly crispy, with melted cheese forming a bubbly, caramelized crust. It sits in a pool of vibrant red marinara sauce, and is garnished with finely chopped fresh parsley, adding a pop of green. The overall look is hearty, rustic, and appetizing—evoking the warmth of a home-cooked Italian meal.

INGREDIENTS

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Cheese Filing
  • Pasta

STEPS

  • Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a pan.
  • Sauté onion until soft (about 5 minutes).
  • Add garlic, cook 1 minute.
  • Add ground beef, cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed
  • Stir in tomato paste, then crushed tomatoes.
  • Season with salt, pepper, oregano, basil.
  • Simmer uncovered for 20–30 minutes until thickened.
